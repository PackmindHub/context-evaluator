# Plan: Packmind Promotion After Remediation

## Context

Context Evaluator is a marketing tool to help developers understand gaps in their AI agent documentation. After remediation, it can create new **coding standards** (outputType: `"standard"`) and **skills** (outputType: `"skill"`). These artifacts are exactly what [Packmind](https://packmind.com) helps teams manage at scale — centralizing, governing, and distributing the engineering playbook across repos.

The goal is to surface a contextual Packmind promotion when these artifacts have been created, making users aware they can go further: manage, distribute, and govern these artifacts with Packmind instead of manually copying markdown files across repos.

## What We're Building

Two promotion surfaces (both only shown when standards or skills were created):
1. **In-card banner** — Inside each expanded `RemediationHistoryCard`, below the action summary
2. **Persistent section** — At the bottom of the `RemediateTab`, when any completed remediation produced standards/skills

Plus one new modal:
- **`PackmindProductTourModal`** — Placeholder iframe container for the embedded product tour (content to be filled later)
